跳过开机引导
adb shell settings put global device_provisioned 1
adb reboot
原生机器跳过开机引导
adb shell settings put secure user_setup_complete 1
adb shell settings put global device_provisioned 1

log输出-附加缓冲区：adb logcat -b

radio — 查看包含在无线/电话相关的缓冲区消息

events — 查看事件相关的消息

main — 查看主缓冲区 (默认缓冲区)
